Our Story

Welcome to 2600 Adventure, your ultimate guide to exploring Baguio City's rich culture, delicious cuisine, and breathtaking attractions. We're passionate about helping visitors and locals alike discover the best experiences this beautiful mountain city has to offer.

We created this project for Techno Peach as part of our web development initiative, combining our love for Baguio with our technical skills to build a comprehensive travel resource.

Our mission is to provide accurate, up-to-date information about Baguio's hotels, restaurants, attractions, and nightlife, making trip planning easier and more enjoyable for everyone.

Our Vision

We believe that everyone should have access to reliable information when planning their Baguio adventures. Our platform is designed to be user-friendly, comprehensive, and constantly updated with the latest information about the city.

As we continue to grow, we aim to add more features like user reviews, personalized recommendations, and interactive maps to make your Baguio experience even better.
